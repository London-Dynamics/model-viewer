LD Clipboard keeps copied parts in memory. Use copyPart() and paste() at the placement cursor. Floor items paste on the floor; wall items require a valid wall snap.

Copy / Paste

Select a part, click Copy (or Ctrl/Cmd+C), move the pointer, then Paste (or Ctrl/Cmd+V). Undo and redo include pasted parts.

Interactive Duplicate

copyPart(uuid, { interactive: true }) shows a semi-transparent ghost on top of the scene. Click to commit; press Escape to cancel.
