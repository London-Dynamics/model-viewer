LD Transforms by London Dynamics. This page showcases how you can control parts of the model on the fly

Transform Geometry

Dynamicly alter position, rotation, and scale of geometry.

Transform Scene

Dynamicly alter position, rotation, and scale of entire scene.

Rotation Controls (Y Axis)

Select an object and drag the floor disc to rotate around Y.

Align and Distribute (Floor)

Multi-select floor-placed parts (Shift+click) and call alignObjects(action) on world X/Z. Each action records one undo step.

Align and Distribute (Wall)

Place wall-snap parts (e.g. from LD Modular), select 2+ on the same wall, then align along the wall horizontally and by height (Y).
